基于苹果操作系统的电子音乐共创工作坊

Electronic Music Co-Creation Workshop Based on Apple Operation System

Date: JAN 16, 2025

Place: Beijing, China

Content: This electronic music co creation workshop provides an open creative platform for electronic music enthusiasts and practitioners, encouraging participants to explore diverse music applications and share their works and inspirations on Apple operating systems (iOS, macOS, iPadOS, VisionOS, etc.) platforms. The workshop provides a space for co creation, discussion, and sharing, aiming to stimulate creative potential and promote in-depth communication and mutual inspiration in electronic music creation.

Flowing Sound Workshop

Date: DEC 21, 2024

Place: Pingshan Art Museum, Pingshan District, Shenzhen, China

Content: In this workshop, urban residents will explore the flowing sounds on the streets of Shenzhen, from residential alleys to art museums, from speakers to radio speakers, and feel the vitality from Shenzhen together, exploring daily life from different perspectives.

城市声境：城市声音日志的交互数据可视化工作坊

Exploring Urban Soundscapes: Interactive Data Visualization with "Urban Sound Diary" Workshop

Date: NOV 22, 2024

Place: Southern University of Science and Technology, Shenzhen, China

Content: This hands-on workshop aims to engage participants in exploring and manipulating urban sound data using the "Urban Sound Diary" app. Through practical exercises, attendees will gain an understanding of the relationship between sound, urban life, computational sound analysis, and data visualization techniques.

智能化乐器创新设计工作坊

Innovative Instrument Design International Workshop

Date: SEP 16 - 20, 2019

Place: Central Conservatory of Music, No.43 Baojia Street, Xicheng District, Beijing, China

Content: This workshop focus on the traditional musical instrument - Duxianqin, which is popular in southern Guangxi and Vietnam, and explore the innovative design of Chinese traditional instruments using digital technology. The participants' work revolves around the shape design of musical instruments, sound synthesis, sensor data collection, interaction design, and other aspects.

Presented by Central Conservatory of Music & Queen Mary University of London, Media Art Technology
