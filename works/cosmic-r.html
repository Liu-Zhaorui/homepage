SURPPOTED BY

Cosmic-R originated from a design workshop aimed at exploring innpvative ways for blind or deaf people to better perceive, perform and create music, as well as a workshop approach to design innovative music installations through co-creation activities.

We used an embodied approach that analogized abstract sound modulation activities to finger movements, creating an intuitive interface for blind users to operate in real-time. The final demo was about the size of an A4 paper and designed for two-handed play. 50 touch-sensitive capacitive sensors, 2 pressure sensors, 10 piezoelectric ceramic vibration sensors, and a joystick were applied to construct the tangible interface. Collaborating with blind advisors, multiple soundscapes were produced and modulated using Ableton Live in combination with Max/Msp, and were matched with corresponding tangible textures such as grid, tracks and arcs. The size, length, and undulations of the textures metaphorically represented musical elements such as pitch, timbre, and rhythm.
