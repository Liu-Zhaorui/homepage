PASS BY 路过

Yangtze River Biodiversity - Fixed Media Work

2023.10--2023.11

MY ROLE

Sound Designer

TOOLS

Ableton Live

SURPPOTED BY

The work presents the realistic digital art of three rare creatures in the Yangtze River Basin: the Yangtze Finless Porpoise（长江江豚）, the White-lipped Deer（白唇鹿）, and the Crested Ibis（朱鹮）. When the audience passes by the work, these creatures also pass by them, forming a mirror image of association and force, and the voices of creatures and humans are intertwined and echoing here. At this moment, we are not only viewers, but also listeners and participants.
