SURPPOTED BY

Nowadays, the requirements for acoustic environment quality in recording are increasingly high. How to design acoustic spaces that meet recording standards based on different building environments needs to consider various factors within the space. Developing and designing a web-based virtual soundfield simulation platform can assist designers in overall control of soundfield design with lower costs and simple operations, thereby meeting various design needs of designers.

This project proposes a design scheme based on three.js, node.js and Google Resolution Audio SDK spatial audio playback technology. Users can modify the properties of the soundfield on the web and receive real-time sound feedback. The verification experiment results show that the virtual soundfield simulation platform can provide good real-time simulation effects and the human-machine interface operation is simple, which has wide application and promotion value.
