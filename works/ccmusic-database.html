A multi-functional music data sharing platform for Computational Musicology research

CCMusic-database, an open data platform for general and Chinese music technology research. Most of the data on the platform is about computational musicology and Music Information Retrieval (MIR) at this stage. It is a platform that has a large collection of publicly available high-quality databases and datasets. The tasks range from singing voice synthesis, automatic singing evaluation to instrument timbre modeling. The vision of the platform is to provide a great convenience for music technology and also to become a link between researchers, dataset builders and even the public (e.g., musicians), which will greatly benefit the whole research domain.

Currently, hundreds of users have used the database for academic research.
