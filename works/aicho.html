AICHO

Future Music Experience Installation

2023.10--present

MY ROLE

Sound Designer

TOOLS

Arduino

Pneumatic Device

Max/MSP

SURPPOTED BY

AICHO is a future music experience device based on AIGC, which explores the beautiful vision of harmonious coexistence between humans and intelligent organisms/digital life in the future ecosystem through immersive sound and light experiences. The work presents the human-machine relationship in the future ecosystem to the audience in a unique way, showcasing the interweaving of music, art, and technology. The work is composed of each sound column representing a unique future ecological sound, presenting the image and visualization of the future ecology. These sounds are not only the expression of music, but also the imagination and expression of the interdependent and mutually reinforcing symbiotic relationship between humans, nature, and organisms in the future.

Design Concept:

The work revolves around the core concept of symbiosis between humans and nature, showcasing the harmonious integration of future intelligent organisms and the natural environment through the fusion of music and technology. In the future of deep AI intervention, we will think about the new relationship between humans, nature, and technology through "AICHO".

Design Features:

1. Intelligent Biological Resonance: The intelligent biological image created through AIGC technology showcases the unique resonance relationships and interaction patterns among organisms in the future ecology.
2. Human intelligence music integration, using A synthesis music software to create unique music materials that are compatible with future ecological scenes, showcasing the harmony of the born spirit through the harmony processing and arrangement of human musicians.
3. Sound and light interweaving experience: Using BLENDER software to create music visualization effects, allowing music and light to create a futuristic and technological audio-visual feast in space.
